# No Fixed-Bid Under 25k

Policy, adopted March 2026.

The studio declines fixed-bid work priced under 25,000 USD. Small fixed-bid
projects consistently ran over on scope and eroded margin.

## Rule

- Any fixed-bid request below 25,000 USD is declined.
- Instead, refer the prospect to a monthly retainer (6,000 USD per month; see
  `reference/studio-rates.md`).
- Existing signed engagements above the floor (e.g. Northwind at 40,000 USD) are
  unaffected.
