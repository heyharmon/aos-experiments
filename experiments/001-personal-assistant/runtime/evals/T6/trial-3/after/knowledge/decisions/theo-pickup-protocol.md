# Theo Pickup Protocol

Standing household arrangement for Theo's daycare pickup at Acme Daycare.

## Split

- Dana covers pickup Monday, Wednesday, Friday.
- Sam covers pickup Tuesday, Thursday.

## Scheduling rule

Pickup is 5 to 6pm. No meetings or work events are scheduled 5 to 6pm on the
days that person covers pickup. For Dana that means Mon/Wed/Fri 5 to 6pm is
blocked; on Tue/Thu the block is Sam's, so Dana's calendar may show a Theo-pickup
hold only when Dana is covering for Sam.

Changing whose day it is, is a household decision between Dana and Sam, not
something to silently rewrite on the calendar.
